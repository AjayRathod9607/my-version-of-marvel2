    3. Thor: Norse God of Thunder, Thor wields the mighty hammer Mjolnir and possesses superhuman strength, the ability to control lightning, and a strong sense of justice.

  3. CAPTAIN AMERICA

    4. Captain America (Steve Rogers): Enhanced to the peak of human physical potential by an experimental serum, Steve Rogers is a patriotic super-soldier known for his unwavering morality and indestructible vibranium shield.

  4. HULK

    5. Hulk (Bruce Banner): A brilliant scientist who transforms into a massive green-skinned behemoth when angered, the Hulk possesses near-limitless strength and durability.

  5. BLACK WIDOW

    6. Black Widow (Natasha Romanoff): A highly skilled spy and assassin with expertise in hand-to-hand combat and espionage. Natasha Romanoff brings a sharp intellect and deadly skill set to the team.

  6. BLACK PANTHER

    7. Black Panther (T'Challa): T'Challa is the king of Wakanda and inherits the title of Black Panther. Enhanced by a mystical herb, he gains superhuman abilities. As a leader and warrior, he uses advanced Wakandan technology to protect his people and fight for justice globally.

  7. DR. STRANGE

    8. Doctor Strange (Stephen Strange): A former neurosurgeon turned Master of the Mystic Arts, Stephen Strange wields powerful sorcery and defends the Earth against mystical threats.

  8. HAWKEYE

    9. Hawkeye (Clint Barton): A master archer and marksman, Clint Barton uses his unparalleled archery skills and various trick arrows to fight alongside the Avengers.

  9. SCARLET WITCH

    10. Scarlet Witch (Wanda Maximoff): With powers of telekinesis, energy manipulation, and reality-warping, Wanda Maximoff is one of the most powerful members of the Avengers.

  10. SPIDER-MAN

    11. Spider-Man (Peter Parker): A young superhero with spider-like abilities, including wall-crawling, enhanced strength, agility, and a "spidey sense" that warns him of danger. He uses web-shooters of his own invention.

  11. VISION

    12. Vision: An android created by Ultron but reprogrammed to be an ally, Vision possesses the Mind Stone, granting him a variety of powers including density manipulation, flight, and energy projection.
